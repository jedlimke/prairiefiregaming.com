The SAN may not be the superpower it was centuries ago, but your image as a power to be reckoned with, and image as a leader in technology, is strong. Before 'The Attack', the SAN was a powerful and well-educated nation and respected by many for what it was capable of. It's focus on medicine and engineering was second to none and many of the fleet's engineers and doctors came out of SAN universities.

The Salvador is the fleet's only vessel dedicated to medical services – an important role that must not be overlooked.

The Chief Surgeon is the most experienced doctor in the fleet. You're responsible for ensuring the sick and wounded are tended to, and as an advocate for the training of more doctors. You also have some… creative ideas about the use of pharmaceuticals that are worthy of exploration…

The FAS is an old enemy but you have more in common with them now than you do with the New Nations. You are concerned that the President of the Council was born a resident of the FAS – though legally the President is bound to not allow this to affect their neutrality, you should be observant and ensure it doesn't sway their decision-making.

The CPA are old friends, although in more recent times they've gradually been focusing more on themselves and your diplomatic ties have weakened.

Rosal are loud and boorish, but ultimately harmless, and can be useful allies if the FAS are getting their way too often.

Proxima are hard to negotiate with, and their incessant rhetoric is tiresome. You are concerned that the prisoners on the Vulcan may be in poor health and are being taken advantage of for their free labor.
Gliese have an annoying habit of exhibiting a "New Nation" style rebellious spirit, but you try to stay on good terms with them given their control of important resources.

Helvetii is an enigma and a recent addition to the Council. They seem to have the "New Nation spark" but are very open minded and consistent with their neutral political approach. They could prove to be a good negotiator and ally. 

Objectives

• Maintain friendship with the CPA. They are your oldest ally, and you fought alongside them in a series of wars centuries ago against the FAS.

• Ensure that additional doctors are being trained and the sick bays are staffed. There are bound to be many casualties in the war against The Wolves.

• Use your expertise in pharmaceutical science to develop and distribute new drugs to improve the health and quality of life of everyone in the fleet. You may find that the scientists at the Endeavour could be useful partners in this.

• Keep close connections with the other SAN citizens aboard the Lucas – they could be valuable allies. Ensure that the SAN council member listens to you, you're also part of the SAN!
Council Member

The Council member for the SAN can spend the Team Phase (Phase 2) on either the Salvador or the Lucas, if they wish.
