Prairie Fire Gaming's inaugural megagame brought 90 players together for an unforgettable experience of international diplomacy, alien encounters, and global crisis management.

_Watch the Skies_ put players in control of nations, media organizations, and secret agencies as they navigated first contact with extraterrestrial visitors. Would humanity unite or fracture in the face of the unknown?
