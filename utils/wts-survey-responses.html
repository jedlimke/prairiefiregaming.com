What would you do differently?

Build and maintain sources harder. I think our team started well, but the rapid cycle (holy shit can 30 minutes just evaporate when you're trying to hit deadline) and eventually exhaustion took their toll. (And to be clear, I mean both for our media team and for the other players.) I would also love to play again as humans (any role) and as aliens.

What did you like the least?

Almost too real. Also: had a serious shitty food hangover when I got home and the next day. (My own fault, and mostly a problem with getting old.) Seriously: Thank you so much for the veggie tray and meat and cheese tray and water container. Coffee and hot water/tea options would be very much appreciated next time. I understand not offering a full mid-day or evening meal. Would like to see more women and people of color helping run the game.

What did you like the most?

Very real, but still a game I could have low-risk fun doing crazy things I'd never do in real life. Winning. Knowing so many people. Not knowing so many people. More women than I expected playing. Would like to have seen more people of color, but that is a problem endemic to our community.

What could we have done better?

I know the logistics are already insane, but some sort of deeper post-gave narrative than the relatively quick debrief and awards. A history of some sort of the events of that day. Hope the pictures and videos and notes all turn into something cool beyond advertising for the next event.

Anything else we should know?

This is seriously burned into my psyche; one of the most intense but fun and low-risk experiences I've had since high school 20+ years ago. Reminded me of the best summer camps and immersion experiences. I imagine preparing for and running the game were everything I loved about being a camp counselor that one summer. I just loved it.

What would you do differently?

having knowledge after playing, I would have made different use of the materials provided in the alien space to be more efficient.

What did you like the least?

Being on the alien, I disliked being so far physically removed from the play area and having to sprint to make it places. I felt that it impacted our ability to respond to quickly to events that arose.

What did you like the most?

adapting to the changes of the game events that arose and working together with a diverse group of people. I thought the game concepts and dynamics were very exciting and in this regard it did not disappoint!

What could we have done better?

Four things come to mind. The logistics of the game made it difficult, specifically the physical distance between the aliens area and the main play area. I think this hindered our ability to respond to events and made it difficult for control (alien control, mail carriers, war room, and main control) to operate effectively. Personally, I felt the alien team was forgotten about during the initial briefing time and missed out on the first round of the game. we were not brought to the alien area until approximately 10:20 and were briefed then. i felt we lost out on critical information initially and were playing catch-up because of it, especially in the war room. I was disappointed food/snacks were not provided in the alien area until very late in the game (3/4 pm) . I heard and saw snacks were available in the hall of nations, but was told entering the hall of nations as an alien would have an effect of the game (basically making it off limits unless you wanted to drastically alter the game narrative.) Lastly, I think game rules and rulings could have been worked out/handled better and in a more efficient manner. This is a moderate concern I have considering all the moving parts of a megagame and the sheer randomness of 50+ players makes this very difficult.

Anything else we should know?

I think this was a great time and a massive learning experience as a player and for the control team. I am sure feedback from players will guide your decision making for future games to improve the player experience. It wasn't perfect, but it was a hell of a lot of fun to play! I was not disappointed in the gaming experience and would most definitely play again in the genuinely exciting crazy chaos of another megagame!
