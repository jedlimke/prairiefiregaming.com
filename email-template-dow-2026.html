After a long wait—thank you COVID, life, and everything in between—Prairie Fire Gaming is proud to finally announce we're holding another MEGAGAME in Fargo!

Humanity is on the run.

Saturday, March 7th · Fargo, ND · 6 Hours · 60 Players

A catastrophic attack has left humanity with one warship and a handful of civilian vessels fleeing into deep space. The enemy—the "wolves"—are relentless, closing in from behind. Resources are scarce. Trust is scarcer. And somewhere among your crew... there might be a traitor.

Den of Wolves: Infinite Domain is a cooperative megagame about survival under pressure. Think Battlestar Galactica meets diplomacy meets roleplaying meets high-stakes board gaming meets live-action chaos.

You might be a ship captain coordinating with engineers to keep the lights on. You might be a politician arguing over who gets the last supply of fuel. You might be military personnel trying to defend the fleet from the wolves.

...or you might be a wolf yourself—sabotaging everything from within.

No experience required.
Cosplay encouraged.
Everyone is welcome!
